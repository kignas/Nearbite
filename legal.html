Refund & Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 60 seconds of placement without any charges. Once the restaurant accepts the order and begins preparation, cancellations are not permitted.

Refunds will be initiated immediately if an order is cancelled by the restaurant, or if items are missing/incorrect upon delivery. Refunds take 3-5 business days to reflect in your original payment method.

Terms of Service

By using EatSwada, you agree to provide accurate delivery information. EatSwada acts as a technology platform connecting users with independent restaurant partners in Maynaguri.

Prices and offers are determined by the respective restaurants and are subject to change without prior notice. Abuse of the platform or cash-on-delivery system may result in account suspension.

Privacy Policy

We collect your name, phone number, and delivery address strictly to fulfill your food orders. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Payment details are securely processed by our payment gateway partners (e.g., PhonePe/Razorpay) and are never stored on EatSwada servers.
